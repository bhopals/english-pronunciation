### B Sound

- To say the B Sound, you are gonna put your lips together, and you are gonna turn your voice on (vibrate vocal cord) and make your sound

- B is Stop constant, means

  - 1. Stop in airflow
  - 2. Release

- Examples

  - best. bring, baby, job, peace, price, up
